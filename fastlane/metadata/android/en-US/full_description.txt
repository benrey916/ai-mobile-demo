Note-Taking
- Perfect for students capturing lectures, professionals documenting meetings, doctors recording patient notes, researchers transcribing interviews, and anyone needing accessible hands-free note-taking across all their devices.

Features
- Rich Text Editing - Format your notes with: Title, Bold, italic, and underline text.
- Text alignment (left, right, center)
- Record Voice notes
- Import Audios and Transcribe
- Export Audios to local storage
- Share Audios functionality
- Audio Transcription - Unlimited audio transcriptions after downloading model on your device
- Transcribe Audios in multiple languages
- Simple Search - Find your notes instantly with text search
- Smart Filtering - Filter notes by type (Starred, Voice Notes, Recent)
- Customise Theme - Dark, Light or System
